Our Story is Started

Our story started with a vision to revolutionize the sales tracking industry. Founded in 2010 by a team of passionate entrepreneurs, TrackSales was born out of the need to provide businesses with a more efficient way to monitor and enhance their sales performance. Over the years, we've grown from a small startup into a leading provider of sales tracking solutions, helping thousands of businesses streamline their sales processes and achieve unprecedented growth.

With a commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction, TrackSales continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in sales management. Powered by AI, our team is dedicated to developing cutting-edge tools that not only simplify sales tracking but also provide valuable insights that drive strategic decision-making.

Company Milestones

2010

Track Sales was founded, launching our first version of the sales tracking software.

2012

Reached 1,000 active users, establishing a strong foothold in the market.

2015

Secured $5 million in Series A funding, allowing for significant product development and team expansion.

2017

Introduced AI-powered analytics, revolutionizing how businesses interpret their sales data.

2019

Opened our first international office in London, expanding our global reach.

2021

Achieved 10,000 active users, reflecting our growing impact and customer base.

2023

Launched our mobile app, providing on-the-go sales tracking for busy professionals.
